Product Description

Our eco-friendly food containers are the perfect solution for restaurants, cafes, and food delivery services looking to reduce their environmental impact without compromising on quality. Made from bagasse (sugarcane fiber), these containers are 100% biodegradable and compostable.

Key features:

  • Sturdy construction that holds up well with hot, cold, and liquid foods
  • Microwave safe up to 100°C
  • Oil and water-resistant
  • Naturally off-white color gives a premium, eco-friendly appearance
  • Secure closure to prevent leaks and spills
  • Biodegrades within 90 days in commercial composting facilities

These containers are ideal for a wide range of foods including curries, rice dishes, noodles, salads, and more. The secure closure ensures that food stays fresh during delivery and transport.

By choosing our eco-friendly containers, you're not only providing a better experience for your customers but also contributing to a more sustainable future. Many consumers now actively seek out businesses that use environmentally friendly packaging, giving you a competitive edge in the market.

Available in packs of 100 pieces, with bulk discounts available for larger orders.
