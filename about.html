About SnacPack

We're on a mission to revolutionize food packaging in India with sustainable, high-quality solutions that benefit businesses, customers, and the environment.

Our Story

SnacPack was founded in 2020 by a team of entrepreneurs who saw a growing need for sustainable food packaging solutions in India's rapidly expanding food service industry. What began as a small operation in Mumbai has now grown into a nationwide supplier of premium eco-friendly packaging products.

Our journey started when we noticed the environmental impact of traditional plastic packaging used by restaurants and cafes across the country. We believed there had to be a better way to serve food that didn't compromise on quality or harm the planet.

After extensive research and development, we launched our first line of bagasse food containers, which quickly gained popularity among environmentally conscious businesses. Since then, we've expanded our product range to include a comprehensive selection of sustainable packaging solutions for all food service needs.

Today, SnacPack serves thousands of businesses across India, from small local cafes to large restaurant chains, all united by a commitment to quality and sustainability.

Our Journey

From a small startup to a leading sustainable packaging provider in India, our journey has been marked by continuous growth, innovation, and impact.

The Beginning

2020

SnacPack was founded in Mumbai with a mission to provide sustainable alternatives to plastic food packaging. We launched our first line of bagasse food containers.

Expansion to Delhi & Bangalore

2021

We opened distribution centers in Delhi and Bangalore to better serve customers across India. Our product range expanded to include cups, lids, and cutlery.

Manufacturing Facility

2022

We established our first manufacturing facility in Gujarat, allowing us to control quality and reduce costs while creating local jobs.

Custom Branding Launch

2023

We introduced custom branding services, enabling businesses to showcase their brand identity on our sustainable packaging products.

Nationwide Presence

2024

SnacPack expanded to serve all major cities across India with a network of distribution centers and a comprehensive e-commerce platform.

Innovation Hub

2025

We launched our Innovation Hub in Pune, dedicated to researching and developing next-generation sustainable packaging materials and designs.
